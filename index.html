Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

HabitFlow is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

  • No user registration is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No email addresses are collected
  • No phone numbers are collected
  • No location information is collected
  • No analytics tools are used
  • No advertising services are integrated
  • No third-party SDKs are included
  • No user activity tracking is performed

How HabitFlow Works

HabitFlow allows users to create habits, record progress, and view statistics directly on their devices.

All functionality is performed locally without requiring an internet connection.

Local Data Storage

Habits, completion history, and application settings are stored locally on the user's device.

This information never leaves the device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Internet Access

HabitFlow does not require an internet connection to operate.

All features are available offline.

Third-Party Services

HabitFlow does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Firebase
  • Tracking SDKs
  • Social media integrations
  • External APIs

Children's Privacy

HabitFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no information is collected, the application is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since no personal information is transmitted or stored on external servers, user data remains under the user's control on their device.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no user data for us to access, modify, export, or delete.

Users may remove all locally stored data by clearing application storage or uninstalling the app.
